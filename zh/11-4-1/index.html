    Indicator available Per capita expenditure of central and local government on 'Environmental protection' and 'Recreation, culture and religion'
    Indicator description
    Geographical coverage United Kingdom
    Unit of measurement Percentage (%) and GDP (£) per capita
    Definitions FCS: Framework for Cultural Statistics. Expenditure data has been extracted from annual expenditures of central and local government using UN Classification of the Functions of Government (COFOG) codes for ‘Environmental protection’ GF05 and ‘Recreation, culture and religion’ GF08.
    Calculations Percentage (%) = (Expenditure on Environmental Protection OR Recreation, culture and religion / Total Government Expenditure) OR Per capita = (Expenditure on Environmental Protection OR Recreation, culture and religion / UK Population)
    Other information We will work to identify or develop UK data to meet the global indicator specification.
