    可用指标 Malaria incidence per 100,000
    指标描述
    地域覆盖范围 United Kingdom
    计量单位 Incident rate per 100,000 population/Number of cases/Percentage of total cases
    定义 Incidence of malaria is defined as the number of new cases of malaria per 100,000 people at risk each year.
    计算 (New cases of malaria / Population) * 100,000 | (Malaria deaths / Population) * 100,000 | (New cases of malaria by species / Population) * 100,000
    其他资料 Data follows the UN specification for this indicator. This indicator has not been identified in collaboration with topic experts.
