    Географический охват United Kingdom
    Единица измерения Incident rate per 100,000 population/Number of cases/Percentage of total cases
    Определения Incidence of malaria is defined as the number of new cases of malaria per 100,000 people at risk each year.
    Расчёты (New cases of malaria / Population) * 100,000 | (Malaria deaths / Population) * 100,000 | (New cases of malaria by species / Population) * 100,000
